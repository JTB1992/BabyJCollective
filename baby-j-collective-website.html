Our story

Born from real motherhood.

I’m a mama to two beautiful babies — a spirited two-year-old girl and a sweet six-month-old boy. Both of their names start with J, and they are, quite simply, my whole world. That’s where Baby J Collective got its name.

When I was pregnant with my first, I remember sitting at my laptop completely overwhelmed. The options were endless, the advice was conflicting, and nothing felt tailored to my life, my home, or my budget. I knew there had to be a better way.

Then came my son — born with a heart defect that changed everything. I learned firsthand how quickly circumstances can shift, how much a registry needs to flex with you, and how deeply personal this journey really is. That experience gave Baby J Collective its heart.

Every registry I build is shaped by your unique story — your nursery, your budget, your baby, your life. Because no two families are the same, and you deserve a registry that truly reflects yours.
